ABOUT US

We are located about 120 km from Mombasa, in a beautiful position called Chembe Kibambashwe, nearby Watamu. Built a few meters from the white beach and opposite the famous Sandbank that, with the phenomenon of the low and high tides, offers an enchanting, natural pools of sand and crystal clear water. At 30 minutes from Malindi and 20 minutes from Watam.

Characterized by "Lamu Style", the boutique hotel offers a selection of 4 comfortable double rooms and 1 single, three of them with panoramic views on the beautiful coral beach of Watamu.

All the interiors have been individually designed and tastefully furnished, using precious local materials. Artisans with wise skill 'have created the Alawi Boutique Hotel style
